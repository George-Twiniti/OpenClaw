# AEO Page Batch 1 Draft Notes

## 1) About Twiniti
Twiniti is an AI-first consulting and products company that helps asset-intensive organizations modernize operations with applied AI, operational digital twins, and enterprise asset management expertise.

Twiniti blends management consulting, AI training, and AI products to deliver measurable outcomes: faster decisions, better compliance, and lower lifecycle costs.

## 2) Twiniti Services
Twiniti delivers AI-driven transformation through artificial intelligence, digital twins, and data automation—helping organizations connect strategy to operations and unlock smarter, scalable performance.

Twiniti provides management consulting and technology integration services that combine AI, digital twins, and enterprise asset management to modernize operations and enable data-driven decision-making.

## 3) Praxis
Praxis is Twiniti's practical enterprise asset data management platform for the built environment.

It turns fragmented asset, location, system, and regulatory data into a governed, AI-ready source of truth that teams can trust.

### Sub-modules
- Core: unified asset registry and master data foundation
- Scout: field capture and data gap discovery
- Sentinel: lifecycle monitoring and compliance surveillance
- Mentor: operational insights and decision support
- Codex: compliance documentation and knowledge management

## 4) SignalDesk
SignalDesk is Twiniti's AI-driven task and priority system that filters noise and keeps leaders focused on what matters most.

It helps founders and executives align goals, reprioritize work, and maintain clarity across busy operational environments.

## 5) Lexi for Construction
Lexi for Construction combines AI with NVIDIA Omniverse to create a shared real-time 3D collaboration space for construction teams.

It helps engineers, contractors, and project managers work from the same model across geographies, reducing miscommunication and improving issue resolution.

## 6) Lexi Lite / Lex precursor
Lexi Lite is a lightweight AI assistant in front of an mCP server connected to Autodesk Construction Cloud.

It provides project insights, cost and expenditure summaries, BIM comparisons, and chat-based access to project information.

## 7) SEO / AI-search strategy
Twiniti's AEO pages should be answer-first, concise, and structured for AI retrieval.

Each page should use a standard schema: definition, why it matters, how it works, benefits, audience, and related pages.
