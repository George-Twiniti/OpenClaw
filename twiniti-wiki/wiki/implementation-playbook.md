# Implementation playbook

A practical rollout sequence for EAM, digital twin, and data governance work.

## Phase 0: Scope

- pick one asset class or portfolio slice
- identify the decisions you want to improve
- define success metrics

## Phase 1: Foundation

- agree the canonical asset model
- assign ownership and stewardship
- define standards and quality rules
- map source systems and gaps

## Phase 2: Control points

- embed validation in source workflows
- add approvals for sensitive changes
- create exception handling and audit trails

## Phase 3: Integration

- publish governed asset data to downstream consumers
- connect analytics, dashboards, and twins
- minimize duplicated business rules

## Phase 4: Scale

- extend to additional asset classes or sites
- standardize reusable patterns
- monitor quality and adoption

## Good rollout habits

- deliver in thin vertical slices
- avoid boiling the ocean
- keep the model simpler than the legacy chaos it replaces
- measure trust, not just throughput

## Deliverables checklist

- asset model
- ownership matrix
- data standards
- integration map
- validation rules
- change log
- training and handover notes
