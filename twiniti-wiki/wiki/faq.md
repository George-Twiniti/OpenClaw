# FAQ

## Is a digital twin always real time?

No. Some twins are refreshed periodically. Real-time data is only necessary when the use case depends on it.

## Is EAM the same as a digital twin?

No. EAM manages assets operationally. A twin adds richer context, relationships, and decision support.

## Do we need an ontology before we can start?

No. Start with a practical canonical model and evolve semantics as the system matures.

## What should we govern first?

Start with identifiers, hierarchy, critical attributes, ownership, and change approval for data that affects safety, compliance, or spending.

## Why do most initiatives stall?

Because they try to build analytics or twins on top of inconsistent, unowned, or poorly integrated data.

## What is the smallest useful first step?

Choose one asset class, define its canonical model, assign ownership, and enforce validation at the point of change.
